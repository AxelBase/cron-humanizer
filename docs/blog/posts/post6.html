Previewing Next Run Times

Published: 12 November 2025 • AxelBase

The ultimate test of a cron expression: “When does it actually run?”

Why Preview Matters

Humanized descriptions are great — but nothing beats seeing the exact timestamps.

  • Confirms timezone handling
  • Reveals off-by-one errors
  • Prevents “it didn’t run” incidents

Local Time Is King

UTC previews are useless in Johannesburg or Tokyo. Always convert to Intl.DateTimeFormat().resolvedOptions().timeZone.

Show Multiple Runs

Display at least 5–10 future executions. For * * * * *, show next 10 minutes. For monthly jobs, show next 3 months.

Implementation

Use cron-parser with currentDate and tz options. Format with date-fns for consistency.

See it run. Trust your schedule.
